Requires Xenia Server v4.0+. Older servers will not be able to connect. 

-------

Xenia is secure workplace messaging from behind your firewall.

- Discuss topics in private groups, one-to-one or team-wide
- Easily share and view image files 
- Connect in-house systems with webhooks and Slack-compatible integrations

To use this app, you need a URL for a Xenia server.
